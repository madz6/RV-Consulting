# PulseGuard service description (SYNTHETIC CASE DATA)

**Prepared by:** NordMach product management, 2026-05

PulseGuard is a predictive-maintenance service for NordMach industrial
compressors and rotary equipment:

- Retrofit sensor kit per machine (vibration, temperature, load, run-hours)
- Cloud analytics that predict bearing, valve and motor failures 2–6 weeks out
- Automated intervention scheduling integrated with NordMach field service
- Monthly reliability report per site

**Pilot results (3 enterprise accounts, 86 machines, 18 months):**

- Unplanned downtime reduced by ~35% versus each account's prior-year baseline
- Baseline unplanned downtime across the pilot fleet averaged 30 hours per
  machine per year
- Sensor kit and onboarding cost NordMach approximately £900 per machine
- Ongoing connectivity and cloud cost is approximately £6 per machine per month

**Service delivery:** one dedicated service engineer is required per ~200
connected machines (fully loaded cost ~£58,000/year). The PulseGuard platform
carries a fixed operating cost of ~£380,000/year at current scale.

**Channel:** direct sales only at launch. No distributor involvement.

**Caveats from engineering:** pilot accounts were hand-picked, well-maintained
sites; results at poorly maintained plants are unproven. Mid-market sites have
not been piloted at all.
