# Interview excerpt — Sales Director, NordMach (SYNTHETIC CASE DATA)

**Interviewed:** 2026-06-10 · **Interviewer:** engagement team

> "Every customer I talk to wants this. Outcome pricing is the obvious answer —
> we share the downtime we save, everyone wins. All of our customers will
> accept outcome pricing; I've not heard a single objection."

> "The enterprise accounts negotiate hard on hardware but they don't blink at
> service fees when the value story is clear. Mid-market is more price
> sensitive, but the machine count is where the volume is."

> "One thing — my incentive plan is revenue-based. If we bundle PulseGuard into
> the equipment price for free-ish, my team loses the commission line, and
> honestly they'll stop selling it."

> "Whatever we do, don't make me quote a different price at every plant. My
> reps need one sheet they can explain in five minutes."

**Interviewer note:** the claim that *all* customers will accept outcome
pricing was not supported by any customer research the sales director could
point to; treat as an internal belief.
