# Interview excerpt — Finance Director, NordMach (SYNTHETIC CASE DATA)

**Interviewed:** 2026-06-11 · **Interviewer:** engagement team

> "The Doncaster investment has used most of our headroom. I can fund sensor
> kits for early adopters, but I cannot carry £900 a machine across a thousand
> machines and wait three years for payback. Contribution has to turn positive
> inside eighteen months."

> "I am uncomfortable with revenue that moves with someone else's downtime
> measurement. If we do anything performance-linked, it needs a hard cap, a
> floor and an agreed measurement protocol, or the auditors and I will have a
> long year."

> "Bundling worries me differently — it buries the service revenue inside the
> hardware line, we can't see the margin, and when hardware pricing gets
> squeezed the service goes with it."

> "A subscription we can forecast. Per-machine we can forecast. Those are the
> shapes I can plan cash around."
