# Interview excerpt — Head of Field Service, NordMach (SYNTHETIC CASE DATA)

**Interviewed:** 2026-06-12 · **Interviewer:** engagement team

> "The pilot worked because we picked three well-run sites and put our best
> engineer on it. At a badly maintained plant the model flags everything and
> the customer stops trusting the alerts. I would not promise a 35% downtime
> reduction to every plant on day one."

> "Capacity is my constraint. One engineer covers about two hundred connected
> machines. If sales connects five hundred machines in the first quarter I
> physically cannot serve them — onboarding has to be phased."

> "Billing per connected machine is easy for us to administer, we already track
> connections. Anything performance-linked means we need an agreed downtime
> measurement per site, which is a project in itself."
