# Interview excerpt — Plant Manager, mid-market customer "Account M7" (SYNTHETIC CASE DATA)

**Interviewed:** 2026-06-17 · **Interviewer:** engagement team
**Context:** single site, 9 NordMach machines; did not participate in the pilot.

> "Honestly, we don't measure downtime cost per hour. When a compressor stops
> we lose some shifts and everyone runs around — I couldn't give you a number
> an accountant would sign."

> "Performance-linked billing is a non-starter for me. I'm not agreeing a
> formula where NordMach's own system decides how much I owe them. My owner
> would laugh me out of the room."

> "What I can do is a fixed monthly amount I can put in the budget. If it's
> predictable and it clearly costs less than one bad breakdown a year, that's
> a conversation I can win internally."

> "And keep it simple — one price for the plant. I've got nine machines; I'm
> not doing spreadsheet gymnastics about which six to connect."
